UMBRA

Where Beauty Meets Terror

A VR horror-collection game where your greed for rare treasures plunges the world into darkness, awakening shadow creatures that hunt you based on your deepest fears.

Discover Gameplay Key Features

A World of Contrasts

In Umbra, you explore a breathtakingly beautiful world using unique gorilla-like movement mechanics. But as you collect the precious crystal "Echoes," the world transforms around you, becoming progressively darker and more terrifying.

The more valuable your collection grows, the more dangerous your journey becomes as the shadow creatures called the Shade awaken to hunt you.

The Core Gameplay Loop

1

Explore & Collect

Use intuitive gorilla-like VR movement to traverse a vast, beautiful world. Find and collect crystal "Echoes" of increasing rarity and size.

2

Risk & Reward

Each Echo you collect makes the world darker and more terrifying. Larger treasures increase the danger as the shadow creatures called the Shade begin hunting you.

3

Adapt & Survive

The Shade adapt to your fears, creating unique horror experiences. Use VR climbing and fleeing mechanics to escape while carrying your precious collection.
